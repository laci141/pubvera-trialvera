🔬 Pubvera · Trialvera

Search, filter and analyse clinical trials — registry data from ClinicalTrials.gov, enriched with PubMed, OpenAlex & FDA FAERS safety signals. Keyless and open, 30+ commands in six groups.

Your key is stored only in this browser (localStorage) and sent per-request via the X-LLM-Key header. Never logged or persisted server-side. With a key, an AI synthesis panel (summary · key points · caveats) is added on top of every result — informational only, not medical advice.
